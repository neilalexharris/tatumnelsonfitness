THE FOOTBALL SPEED COACH.

Why Speed Training?


Speedwork and sprint training are essential components of football. In the ever increasingly competitive world of sport, the right speed training and sprint technique will give your child the edge, and take their game to the next level.

Through my “7 Point Speed Training Programme". I incorporate all of the speed training your child needs from sprint technique and focussed specific speed work to acceleration, change of direction, plyometrics, and everything in between.

In addition to speed and sprint work I include specific training tailored to your child to build and maintain the fitness base required for football. This includes football focussed conditioning work, circuit training and injury prevention which are all key to longevity and continuity of football training.

I will analyse their sprint technique, make any necessary adaptations, apply my speed programme and I will improve your child’s speed.

Why Speed Training With Me?


Drawing on my background as an international GB sprinter, when I work with your child, I apply the sprint techniques and speed training to their football that I have learned covering every element of speedwork needed to improve the performance in their game.

I started sprinting at the age of 11, competed for England by the age of 13 and was one of the fastest girls in the world by the age of 14. I have trained and competed at the highest level of sprinting being fortunate enough to travel the world gaining invaluable and incomparable knowledge. I have trained alongside some of the greatest coaches and athletes in the world and I now place all of this knowledge into your child’s speed training with me.

Sessions for Children 6 to 15yrs+


Please contact me directly for prices. There is the option for 1-2-1 sessions, 2-1-1 sessions or 3-2-1- sessions. Thereafter you would need to attend a group session.

There is a chargeable initial session and analysis. After this analysis I can create a bespoke programme tailored to your child’s specific requirements.

Thereafter it is block-bookings.
Block bookings are to ensure continuity of training and commitment so results can be seen. Optimum results cannot be achieved after 1 session.

If you are referred by an Academy or Development Centre that I work with then there is only the option to block-book, as your technique will have been analysed by my colleagues within football.
